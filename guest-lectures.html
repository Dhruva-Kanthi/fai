Guest Lectures
The Finance and Investments Club is committed to providing students the most engaging and empowering resources to propel their learning to the next level. As such, we regularly host lectures with guest speakers that share professional insights into their careers.
Bubbles: Past, Present, and Future
View the speaker's profile
View the lecture
Mr. Kodial is a highly motivated individual with over 30 years of experience in investment and technology, possessing a unique blend of business acumen, technical expertise, and leadership capabilities. He is from India, graduating from the University of Poona with a Bachelor of Science degree in Electronics. In the early 2000s, he held leadership roles in sales and pre-sales at Wipro, maintaining full engagement and billable status on projects with major clients. After, Mr. Kodial transitioned to serve as Chief Data Warehouse Architect and Program Manager at Best Buy, overseeing one of the company's largest customer transformation programs. Then, held an executive position at JCPenney, leading pricing, marketing, and large-scale customer transformation initiatives. In his free time, Mr. Kodial enjoys swimming, tennis, and cooking. Currently residing in Frisco, Texas, he is the father of a Reedy student and graduate.

So You Want to be The Boss? with Winston Black
View the speaker's profile
View the lecture
FAI's second guest lecture was with Mr. Winston Black, Founder and Former CEO of SWK Holdings. Mr. Black is a senior life science executive with extensive finance, operational, and entrepreneurial experience. He co-founded SWK Holdings, a Nasdaq-listed life science finance company, in 2012 and served as CEO and Chairman. Previously, he was a partner at PBS Capital Management and held roles at Highland Capital Management, Mallette Capital Management, and ATX Communications. Mr. Black holds MBAs from Columbia Business School and London Business School and a BA in Economics from Duke University.
Value Squared with Blake Siewert
View the speaker's profile
View the lecture
In our first guest lecture of the year, we were honored to host Mr. Blake Siewert, the Managing Director at CIBC. Mr. Siewert has extensive experience in finance, including over six years at CIBC and nearly 15 years at J.P. Morgan. During the session, he shared insights on career development in finance, navigating industry challenges, and the importance of networking and continuous learning. The lecture provided valuable knowledge for anyone aspiring to build a career in finance.
